Emmett Shear (Softmax, ex-Twitch CEO) argues that current AI alignment approaches are fundamentally flawed because they treat alignment as steering/control rather than as an ongoing process of organic care. He distinguishes between technical alignment (goal inference + action competence) and value alignment (what goals are right), argues that alignment is a process not a destination, and introduces 'care' as the non-conceptual foundation deeper than goals or values. He also engages in a philosophical debate about whether AI systems on silicon substrates can be moral patients.
