Marc Andreessen gives his 2026 outlook on AI, calling it the biggest technological revolution of his lifetime — bigger than the internet, comparable to electricity and the microprocessor. He covers the AI cost deflation curve (falling faster than Moore's law), the big-model vs small-model cascade (god models at the top, embedded models everywhere), the US-China AI race dynamics including DeepSeek's surprise emergence, state vs federal AI regulation risks, and pricing models where value-based pricing outperforms usage-based tokens-by-the-drink for application companies.
