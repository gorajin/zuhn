Columbia professor Vishal Misra explains his mathematical proof that LLMs are Bayesian inference machines doing correlation (Shannon entropy), not causation (Kolmogorov complexity). He argues AGI requires two breakthroughs beyond scale: continual learning (weight plasticity) and causal modeling (simulation), neither of which current transformer architectures can achieve.
