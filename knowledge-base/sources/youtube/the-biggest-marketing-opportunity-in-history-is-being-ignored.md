GaryVee argues that organic social media remains the most underpriced marketing opportunity in history because posting is free, yet most businesses drastically under-invest in content. He warns that AI-driven search (ChatGPT replacing Google) will disrupt paid acquisition channels, and urges businesses to build brand through consistent social posting while overcoming the fear of public judgment.
