Pursuit of Wonder examines Carl Jung's theory of synchronicity — meaningful coincidences that involve acausal connections between internal psychic states and external events. The video traces Jung's concept from its four defining criteria through his metaphysical framework of 'unus mundus' (unified substrate of mind and matter), contextualizing it against historical parallels in Daoism, Hinduism, and Stoic philosophy, while acknowledging its unfalsifiability.
