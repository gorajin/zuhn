Max Konyi provides a systematic tour of all 12 chromatic scale degrees as subjective feeling states rather than pitch labels. He demonstrates how each degree has a unique emotional character — from the stability of degree 1 to the desperation of flat 2 — organized on a circle where adjacent degrees feel most similar and opposite degrees feel most different. The key pedagogical insight: melodic proximity (pitch closeness) and harmonic proximity (feeling closeness) are completely different dimensions.
