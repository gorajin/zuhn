Atlassian CEO Mike Cannon-Brookes and a16z's Alex Rampell discuss the so-called SaaS apocalypse, arguing that SaaS companies fall into three distinct categories based on whether seat pricing is tied to outcomes. They explore why vibe coding won't replace enterprise software, how businesses are actually collections of processes (not systems of record), and the critical UX challenge of building trust in human-agent collaborative workflows.
