Loh tunes his X feed to right-leaning viewpoints and his Facebook to left-leaning viewpoints, reading both every day. His goal isn't to find truth in either side but to identify where they disagree and understand why — using disagreement as the signal for underlying values differences.

With only a handful of major AI providers (Claude, GPT, Gemini, DeepSeek), the world's 7.5 billion viewpoints are being compressed into perhaps 5 perspectives. AI's particular danger is that it sounds complete and authoritative — you may think you have the entire story on a controversial situation, but you don't. The practical defense: systematically expose yourself to viewpoints that contradict your defaults, and use AI to build your own mental model rather than accepting its conclusions wholesale.
